**Topic: History of Texas**
- Early beginnings through present day

- Texas Revolution

- Important issues, events, individuals of 19th-21st centuries

- Spanish settlements and missions
- Spanish colonial and Mexican governments
- Pledge to the Texas Flag
- Contributions of various racial, ethnic, religious groups
- Impact of science and technology
- Critical thinking: cause-effect, compare-contrast, generalizations, predictions
