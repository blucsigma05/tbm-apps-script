## STAAR Science Note
5th grade is when STAAR Science is tested. This 4th grade curriculum builds the foundation.
Campus 5th grade Science STAAR scores (2024-25): only **5% Masters** (vs. 21% district, 17% state) — 
the largest campus gap in any subject. Aggressive preparation starting now (4th grade) is warranted.
Key 5th grade crossover topics built here: matter, energy transfer, ecosystems, earth patterns.
