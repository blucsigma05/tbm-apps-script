Power Scan

Wolfkid Intelligence Division
Every hero has a unique power profile. Wolfkid needs to know his stats before he can level up.

This scan reads 10 core brain powers. There are no right or wrong answers — just honest ones. Some powers will be high. Some will be lower. That's how every hero works. Even the strongest ones have powers they're still building.

The scan has 3 parts:
1. Rate your 10 powers (1 = still building, 5 = superpower)
2. When your powers are at full strength
3. When your powers glitch + what causes it
Part 1 of 3 — Power Levels
Part 2 of 3 — Full Power Mode

When Your Powers Are Maxed Out

Think about a time your brain was running perfectly. You were focused, getting stuff done, feeling good. What were you doing? What did it feel like?
Example: "I'm drawing my comic and I don't even notice time passing. I can hold the whole story in my head. I feel calm and locked in."

When Your Powers Glitch

Now think about the opposite. When your brain feels foggy, frustrated, or stuck. What happens? What does it feel like?
Example: "I can't sit still. I keep starting things but not finishing. I get mad at myself and want to quit."
Part 3 of 3 — Power Drains

What Drains Your Energy?

What situations, feelings, or environments make your brain switch into glitch mode? These are your power drains — knowing them is how you learn to avoid or prepare for them.
Example: "Being told I have 5 minutes left. Too much noise. Being hungry. When someone changes the plan at the last second."

Your Secret Recharge

What helps you get your powers back when they glitch? What recharges you?
Example: "Drawing. Going outside. Having snack time. Listening to music. Being left alone for a few minutes."
